# Content Plan — Bookshelf

## Strategy Overview

**Goal:** Build Myanmar's go-to digital book platform via Telegram. Start with 50 curated books, grow to 200+ in 6 months through author partnerships and community curation.

**Audience:** Myanmar readers aged 18-45 on Telegram. Burmese-first, English as secondary. Mobile-only. 3G-reliant segment is significant.

---

### Book Curation Criteria
- Mix of genres: at least 3 books per genre category
- At least 40% Burmese-language content
- Public domain where possible (no copyright issues)

## Phase 2: Growth (Month 1-3)

### Content Goals
- Reach 100 books
- Partner with 5 independent Myanmar authors
- Create 3 "Collection" series (e.g., "Burmese Classics", "Startup Library", "Myanmar History")

### Author Partnerships
- 70/30 revenue share on premium books
- Featured author spot in channel weekly
- Author-hosted Q&A in Telegram channel

### Collection Ideas

**Burmese Classics (10 books)**
- Curated vintage Burmese literature
- All free tier
- Marketing hook: "Read the books your parents grew up with"

**Startup Library (10 books)**
- Lean Startup, Zero to One, Atomic Habits + 7 more
- Mix Burmese & English business books
- Target young entrepreneurs

**Myanmar History (8 books)**
- From Pagan to modern era
- Partner with history professors
- Premium tier for rare/translated titles

## Phase 3: Scale (Month 3-6)

### Content Goals
- Reach 200 books

- Auto-split revenue

- Approach Myanmar publishing houses

- Bulk licensing deals

### User-Generated Content
- Reading lists (user-curated collections)
- Book recommendations ("If you liked X, try Y")
- Reading challenges ("Read 10 Burmese books this month")

## Content Quality Standards

### Book Requirements
- Clean, readable PDF or EPUB format
- Proper metadata (title, author, language)
- No watermarks from other platforms
- File size under 20MB (3G-friendly)
- Copyright cleared or permission obtained

### Cover Emoji Selection Guide
- Fiction: 📖 🖋️ 🎭 💃 🥂
- History: 🏛️ 📜 🛕 🏯
- Science: 🔬 🔭 🧬 ⚗️
- Self-Help: 🌟 💡 🧘 ⚛️
- Business: 💼 🚀 📊 💰
- Poetry: 🌸 🎵 ✒️ 🕊️
- Philosophy: 🤔 💭 ⚔️
- Pick emojis that evoke the book's mood, not literal covers

Cool tones:  #DBEAFE (blue), #ECFDF5 (green), #F5F3FF (purple), #EFF6FF (indigo)

## Risk Management

### Copyright Issues
- Only public domain or explicitly licensed books
- Keep author permission records
- DMCA takedown process ready
- Legal consultation before launch

### Content Quality
- Review every book before publishing
- Remove books with formatting issues
- User report mechanism for broken files

### Author Disputes
- Clear written agreements with all authors
- Transparent revenue reporting
- Easy opt-out for authors

### Competition
- Facebook book groups → differentiate with Mini App convenience
- Google Play Books → differentiate with Myanmar focus + KBZ Pay
- Physical bookstores → complementary, not competitive
